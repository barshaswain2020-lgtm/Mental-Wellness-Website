What is Mental Wellness?

Mental wellness refers to a state of well-being where an individual can cope with stress, work
  productively, build positive relationships, and make meaningful contributions to society

How does sleep affect your mental health?

Sleep helps regulate mood, memory, and stress levels.
     Poor sleep can increase anxiety, irritability, 
                 and the risk of depression.

One daily habit that supports mental wellness.

Practicing mindfulness or meditation can reduce
stress and improve focus and emotional control.

Why is it important to talk about mental health?

Talking about mental health reduces stigma,promotes
      support-seeking behavior, and helps others
            understand they are not alone.

Can physical health impact mental wellness?

Yes! Regular exercise, healthy eating, and adequate
sleep can boost mood, reduce anxiety, and improve
                overall mental health.

5 Common Mental Health Disorders

1. Depression
Persistent sadness, hopelessness, and
loss of interest in activities.

2. Anxiety Disorders
Includes generalized anxiety, panic
attacks, and phobias —
characterized by constant worry or
fear.

3. Bipolar Disorder
Extreme mood swings from emotional
highs (mania) to lows (depression).

4. Obsessive-Compulsive
Disorder (OCD)
Unwanted, repetitive thoughts
(obsessions) and behaviors
(compulsions).

5. Post-Traumatic Stress
Disorder (PTSD)
Develops after experiencing or
witnessing a traumatic event.
Causes flashbacks, nightmares,
and anxiety.

🌿 Meditation Instructions


(Before Starting) Please follow these steps before starting your meditation:
Choose a calm place

Sit in a quiet and comfortable place where you will not be disturbed.

Sit properly

Sit straight on a chair or on the floor. Keep your back relaxed and your hands on your knees.

Relax your body

Close your eyes gently and loosen your shoulders, neck, and face.

Take deep breaths

Inhale slowly through your nose… Exhale slowly through your mouth. Repeat this 3 times.

Set your meditation time

Enter the number of minutes you want to meditate in the timer box.

Prepare your mind

Let go of all thoughts. Focus only on your breathing. Count down to begin In your mind, slowly count backwards:10-1 Start meditation





Click the Start button and stay calm and present until the timer ends.

About inbloom

Welcome to your personal sanctuary of growth. Here, we believe in nurturing your inner world, one bloom at a time, fostering positivity, peace, and self-discovery.

Our Blooming Story

inbloom began as a dream to create a digital oasis where people could cultivate their emotional well-being. Inspired by the gentle yet powerful growth of nature, our founder envisioned a space where every individual could discover their inner beauty and strength.

What started as a simple idea has grown into a comprehensive platform offering mood tracking, guided meditation with timers, and reflective journaling. Each tool is designed to help you bloom – to grow through self-compassion and positive habits.

Our Blooming Mission
